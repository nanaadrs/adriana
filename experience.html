MY EXPERIENCE

My journey in Kawad Kaki Kadet Polis was an exhilarating experience that taught me discipline, teamwork, and the importance of precision. The rigorous training sessions, which involved mastering complex footwork and commands, were both challenging and rewarding. The highlight of my experience was undoubtedly participating in the school-level competition held at SMK Agama Kedah. The atmosphere was electric, with teams from various schools showcasing their skills and dedication. Our team, fueled by months of intense practice, performed flawlessly, executing each movement with synchronized precision. The moment the results were announced, and our team was declared the third-place winner, was a feeling of immense pride and accomplishment. The hard work, sweat, and countless hours of practice had finally paid off. This achievement not only boosted our confidence but also instilled in us a sense of camaraderie and sportsmanship.

Working as a kuih raya maker was a whirlwind of activity especially when I worked for one month in Ramadhan. Each day was a marathon of measuring, mixing, and molding, as I meticulously crafted a variety of traditional delights. From the delicate crunch of peanut cookies to the buttery richness of pineapple tarts, every bite was a labor of love. The most challenging aspect was maintaining consistency, ensuring each batch was as perfect as the last. Yet, the reward was palpable - the joy on customers' faces as they savored the fruits of my labor. It was a demanding yet fulfilling experience, a testament to the artistry and dedication required to create these beloved treats.

My two-month internship at PKNK's IT Department was a valuable learning experience. I was exposed to various aspects of IT operations, including network administration, software development, and system support. I gained hands-on experience by assisting with tasks such as troubleshooting network issues, configuring servers, and developing simple applications. The supportive and knowledgeable IT team provided guidance and mentorship, allowing me to apply my academic knowledge to real-world scenarios. Through this internship, I developed practical skills, enhanced my problem-solving abilities, and gained a deeper understanding of the IT industry.
